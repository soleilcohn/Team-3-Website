FAQ

Short answers to the most common questions from parents and partners.

How many doses does the necklace hold?

Our current target is ~5 rescue puffs as an emergency bridge until the child reaches their primary inhaler or care.

Is it safe?

It leverages existing, well-established inhaler technology within a miniaturized housing. Final safety depends on regulatory testing with partners.

How do refills work?

Parents can replace the cartridge. Long-term, we plan subscription refills and insurance-bundled options via pharma partners.

Is this available now?

We’re in the concept and partnership phase. Join the waitlist to receive updates on pilots and availability.
