Design

A discreet necklace with a tiny emergency pod delivering ~5 albuterol puffs.

Form Factor

Lightweight pendant; magnetic clasp; hypoallergenic band options.

• Pendant: ~28–32mm
• Weight: under 25g (target)

Cartridge

Mini canister with metered-dose valve; approx. five actuations (rescue-only).

• Tamper-evident
• Child-resistant refill

Use Flow

  1. Twist-unlock pendant
  2. Place to mouth; press
  3. Relock; replace cartridge later

Safety & Usability

  • Human-factors study focused on children and caregivers
  • Clear dose indicator & tactile feedback
  • Drug–device combination testing (bioequivalence)
