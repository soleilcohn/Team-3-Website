Why Us

Kids forget or avoid bulky inhalers. Parents worry about emergencies. We make rescue access effortless—always on, stigma-free, designed for real life.

Problem

Inhalers Get Left Behind

Children with asthma often fail to carry their rescue inhalers due to stigma, forgetfulness, or inconvenience. Traditional inhalers are bulky, easy to misplace, and a visible reminder of “being different.” This leads to dangerous non-compliance, leaving kids vulnerable during sudden asthma attacks when every second counts.

Solution

Wearable Rescue

A discreet necklace that doubles as a portable emergency inhaler. Designed with a child-friendly look, it contains ~5 pre-metered emergency puffs, stored securely and easily accessible. Unlike traditional inhalers left in backpacks, lockers, or at home, the necklace is always on the child, offering instant relief when symptoms strike. Its design emphasizes discretion and normalcy, removing the stigma associated with carrying medication.

Value

Peace of Mind

  • For parents: Confidence that their child is never without fast-acting medication, reducing the constant anxiety of “what if.”
  • For kids: Independence and dignity—no need to feel singled out or embarrassed by carrying a standard inhaler.
  • For health outcomes: Faster access to treatment improves recovery times, reduces ER visits, and supports better long-term asthma management.
